One Mil-Lion

One Mil-Lion is an action-packed top-down shooter where you play as a dragon going up against hordes of lions inside of an arena. The game was made as part of a team during a two-day game jam where I worked on programming, UI and the sound effects for the game.

One Mil-Lion won the Judges' Choice Award
