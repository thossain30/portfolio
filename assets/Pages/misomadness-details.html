Mi So Madness - Try Not to Get Eaten

Mi So Madness is a ramen multiplayer networked game. Play as cup ramen(oni-tan) or bowl ramen(maru-chan) with your friends on Steam or locally through LAN. You fight against your friends in an infinite runner game and only the best players advance to the sumo level where the last one standing is the only one not eaten.

This project allowed me to hone the 3D art skills that I picked up from my 3D modeling course which I previously took. I started off with an orange cut in half which was used as a prop for the background to give the game the fun, party-like aesthetic which we wanted. This was a good warm-up model that taught me useful tips about texturing and UV mapping textures on my models.

After creating the Orange in half, the leads determined that we needed an obstacle that the players needed to overcome. We thought up of foods that would make Ramen taste bad. After some discussion, we settled on a Chocolate bar. After the first iteration of the model, the team leads and I decided to make some of the chocolate look "spoiled" to drive home the fact that it is an obstacle. To further imply that this is something bad to come into contact with, I added a devil face I found and lined it up through UV mapping. The team leads liked this addition and it went on through the final model.

After more iterations consisting of reducing face and vertex count for optimization, we got the model in the game and I was happy to say that it looked great in game. I am satisfied with how the models turned out and was happy that I was able to showcase it during our end-of-the-semester demo.
