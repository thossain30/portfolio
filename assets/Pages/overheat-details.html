Overheat - A Fight That Will Leave Your Hands Hot

Overheat is an action-packed, character fighting game where you play as Axel, a mechanic who wields flame-based attacks. Axel's precious creations have been stolen by his brother for dubious ends. Time for him to take it all back the only way he knows how: in style. Take control of Axel and combo robot enemies into oblivion to take down your brother!

This project was a fun one as it was one of the first games led using Unreal Engine for my club. As a result, it was the first time using Unreal Engine for a lot of us. To assist us, we found a tutorial series on the basics of Unreal Engine.

After learning some of the basics, I decided to take on programming the Neutral Special attack, one of the "special" attacks Axel can use. One challenge to this task was figuring out how to map the visual effect of the attack to the input system. After watching another tutorial and taking the time to understand that, I was able to make the vfx show up when the button for the attack was pressed. However, I could not figure out how to position it on the player character as the attack was a fire tornado that would damage anything around the player within a certain radius.

I decided to consult the Team Lead about this and we both spent approximately an hour figuring this problem out. After experimenting with some math nodes in Unreal's Blueprint system together, we got the attack working properly. Overall, this was a good experience for me as it exposed me to working with Blueprints and showed me the importance of teamwork and peer-programming.
